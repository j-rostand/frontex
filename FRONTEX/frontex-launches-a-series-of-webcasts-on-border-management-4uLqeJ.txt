On Tuesday, Frontex will launch a series of webcasts highlighting the work of the graduates of its unique Joint Master’s in Strategic Border Management programme.In the first live presentation of the “Master’s on the Road” series, Lt. Col Pascal Robin Wolf of the Royal Netherlands Marechaussee will talk about the protection of mental health of border guards in stressful situations. He will also speak about the establishment of a common European Mental Health Management programme for border guards.The presentation will be streamed live in English on YouTube on Tuesday, 12 June, from 10.00 CEST.
