Italian
authorities in Catania, Sicily, have launched an investigation into the
disappearance of a sailor who was taking part in Operation Triton aboard
Norwegian vessel Siem Pilot.Benjamin
Olafsson, 23, was last seen on Monday at 3.30 am in Catania, where Siem Pilot
is docked.Italian police
are conducting a search on land and sea in close cooperation with the Norwegian
police officers aboard Siem Pilot and the Norwegian National Criminal
Investigation Service."Frontex, which
coordinates Operation Triton, will take any necessary measures to support the
Norwegian authorities in this difficult situation," said Frontex Executive
Director Fabrice Leggeri.
