In July,
there were 15 400 detections of illegal border crossings on the four main
migratory routes into the EU as the number of migrants arriving in Italy fell
by more than half from the previous month. Spain continued to see the heaviest
migratory pressure since 2009.The total
number of detections in the first seven months of 2017 fell by two-thirds from the same period of last year to 127 100.
