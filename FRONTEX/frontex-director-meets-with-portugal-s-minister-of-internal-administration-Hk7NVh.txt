
The
Executive Director of Frontex, the European Border and Coast Guard Agency, met
with Portugal’s Minister of Internal Administration and with the National
Director of Portuguese Immigration and Border Service (SEF) on Friday to introduce
the agency’s liaison officer for Portugal and Spain.
Frontex is deploying 11 liaison officers to enhance the
cooperation between the agency and national authorities responsible for border management,
returns and coast guard functions in 30 EU Member States and Schengen Associated Countries.
