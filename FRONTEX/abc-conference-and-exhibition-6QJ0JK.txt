
On October
10-11, 2013, Frontex and the European Commission (DG HOME and DG ENTR) will
hold the 2nd Global Conference and Exhibition on future developments of
Automated Border Control (ABC). The Conference will take place at the Municipal
Stadium of the City of Warsaw (Pepsi Arena). 
This year
the Conference – the only event on a global scale dedicated exclusively to ABC
issues – will gather fifty high calibre speakers (please refer to the final
programme for further details) and two hundred delegates, including government
officials from EU Member States and Third Countries, representatives of EU
institutions and Agencies, twelve research institutes and universities, more
than twenty technology providers and a number of international organisations
and associations. 
Several
sessions of the Conference will be live-streamed via the online platform: www.frontex.tvip.pl
