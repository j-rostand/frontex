
On Tuesday, 16 October 2018,
Frontex launches the second of a series of webcasts on the research of the
graduates of its unique European Joint Master’s in Strategic Border Management
programme.
The event, which will be streamed
live, will feature Superintendent Lars Andreas Lindstøl of the Norwegian
Police, who will talk about “Integrated Border Management (IBM) and Network
Governance”. He will focus on the way the IBM concept can be understood and implemented in each Member
State. 
This Master’s on the Road webcast
will be hosted by the Alexandru Ioan Cuza Police Academy in Bucharest,
Romania. It will be broadcasted at https://vimeo.com/291456148 on 16
October 2018, at 11.00 EEST (Bucharest time) or 10.00 CET (Warsaw time).
