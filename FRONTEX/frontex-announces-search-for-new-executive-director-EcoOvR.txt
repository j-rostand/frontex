The post was left vacant at the end of May
when Ilkka Laitinen, the first Frontex executive director left the agency after 9 years of service. The vacancy was announced in
today’s Official
Journal C 180 A. Among the qualities of candidates the
vacancy enlists:Solid experience preferably in
border management, or else migration or law enforcement;Ability to develop an overall
forward-looking vision for the agency;Proven administrative, management
and communication skills.The executive director will be appointed
by the Management Board on the basis of a shortlist established by the European
Commission.To apply, please consult the Official Journal C 180 A
for the detailed vacancy notice as well as the eligibility and selection
criteria. The closing date for registration is 14 July 2014, 12.00 CET.
