On Monday, Frontex assisted
Greece in transporting 202 migrants on three ferries from the islands of Lesbos
and Chios to Turkey. The operation included 180 escort officers deployed by
Frontex, as well as Greek officers.The
readmission operation is coordinated between the Greek and Turkish authorities
as part of the EU-Turkey agreement. The
Greek authorities are responsible for making sure that all migrants to be
readmitted have been subject to individual decisions taken in full accordance
with EU and international law. Note
