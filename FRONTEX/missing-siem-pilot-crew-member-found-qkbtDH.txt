A member of the crew of the Norwegian vessel Siem Pilot who had gone missing early Monday
morning was found on Wednesday night.Benjamin Olafsson, 23, had disappeared in Catania, Sicily,
where Siem Pilot is docked. After he was found, Olafsson was
reunited with family members who flew to Sicily. He will travel with them back
to Norway in the coming days.“I am glad Benjamin is safe and sound. Benjamin and the rest
of the Siem Pilot crew have done an
outstanding job helping to save thousands of people as part of Operation
Triton,” said Frontex Executive Director Fabrice Leggeri.
Siem Pilot helped
rescue 6 500 migrants in the Mediterranean since it was deployed by Frontex in
June.
