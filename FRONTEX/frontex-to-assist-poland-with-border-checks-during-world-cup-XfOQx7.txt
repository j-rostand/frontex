Frontex, the European Border and Coast Guard Agency, will provide 
border control officers from around Europe to assist Poland to ensure 
safe border passage for football fans heading to Russia for the World 
Cup this month.Thousands of fans are expected to cross Poland by 
car, bus or train on their way to Kaliningrad, Russia. Each will cross 
the Polish border on their way to four matches at the Kaliningrad 
Stadium.
